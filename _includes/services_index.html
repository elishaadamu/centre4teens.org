Our Objectives

Centre for Teens Health and Development (Centre4Teens) is a youth-focused non-profit organization dedicated to supporting the holistic growth of teenagers across Nigeria.

Our Mission

To empower teens with the knowledge, skills, and mindset they need to make informed decisions, adopt healthy habits, and grow into self-reliant, thriving adults.

Our Vision

A world where every teenager is healthy, confident, and empowered to reach their full potential and contribute meaningfully to society.
